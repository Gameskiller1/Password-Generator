7. Cookies Policy

Our website uses cookies to enhance your experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences and settings.

Cookie Types We Use:

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
cookieConsent Stores user's cookie consent preference 1 Year
_ga, _gat, _gid Google Analytics - Analyzes user behavior and traffic patterns (anonymized data) 2 Years
IDE, NID Google AdSense - Delivers personalized advertisements based on user interests 1-2 Years
localStorage Stores user preferences, settings, and generated password history (local only) Until Cleared

How to Control Cookies:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent. You can manage cookies through your browser's privacy settings.
  • Disabling Cookies: If you disable cookies, some features of our website may not function properly. For example, you may need to log in again or some preferences won't be saved.
  • Third-Party Tools: You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Do Not Track: Some browsers support the "Do Not Track" feature. However, we don't currently respond to Do Not Track signals.
⚠️ Important: When you accept our cookie banner, you consent to:
  • Google Analytics collecting anonymized usage data
  • Google AdSense delivering personalized ads
